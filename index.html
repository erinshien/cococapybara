Instructions

Coco is delighted you are looking after her! Please note her special care instructions:

1. Coco loves to eat! Give her a little snack by clicking the feed button.

Be careful though, the more she eats the more it tuckers her out - if she gets too full she'll enter a food coma!

2. Coco loves company! Play with her by clicking the play button.

All of this playing sure does work up her appetite, make sure you feed her to keep her going!

3. Coco needs her rest! Get her to have a nap by clicking the sleep button.

Sleeping by herself too much makes her feel a bit lonely, so do play with her to keep her spirits up!
